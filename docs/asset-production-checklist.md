# Asset Production Checklist

Every coloring image must pass all checks before shipping.

## Artwork
- Warm inviting Jesus expression
- Emotionally readable face
- Soft divine lighting
- Clear silhouette
- No uncanny anatomy

## Segmentation
- Closed vector regions
- No broken fills
- No microscopic tap zones
- Large satisfying fill areas
- Balanced complexity

## Palette
- 10-20 colors max
- Harmonized warm palette
- Good contrast between neighboring regions
- Avoid duplicate shades

## UX
- Looks beautiful while incomplete
- Zoom not required constantly
- Focal points reveal early
- Completion feels rewarding

## Export Files
- final_art.png
- line_art.svg
- regions.svg
- palette.json
- metadata.json
- thumbnail.png
